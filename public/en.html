HeatMapp


Android application developed as a final project for my User Interface Design class at UIUC, in a group with three more students, all focusing only in the app interface. The idea of this app is to gather the location of the users and show it anonymously in a map, so other users can have an idea about how crowded a place is. It is also supposed show pictures taken in the most crowded places around the campus.



What I worked with

  • Android
  • UX and UI prototyping
